Egusi

INGREDIENTS

  1. Palm Oil
  2. Goat Meat
  3. Spices
  4. Dried Fish
  5. Palm Kernel
  6. Garlic and Ginger (optional)
  7. Fresh Pepper

HOW TO PREPARE

  • Boil the Goat meat with spices
  • Boil the Palm Kernel
  • Pour it inside mortar
  • Pound it until the skin is soft
  • Remove the skin snd add water
  • Sieve it and pour the watery content into a pot and boil
  • Add your spices (Salt, Maggi, Garlic and Ginger)
  • Add dried Fish and Goat meat
  • Boil till it is done
